THE HOUSE OF

Indigo Motoring Company creates hand crafted clothing inspired by the transportation modes of past, present and future. By studying details in the vehicle designs that inspire our collections, we create pieces using high grade textiles and thread on both modern and antique machines. INMOCO’s aim is to make clothing in the spirit of “durable high fashion”, by showing our clients classic styles of garments that are strong enough to withstand the test of time both visually and physically. Because, the world needs better cloth.
